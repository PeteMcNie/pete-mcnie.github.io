What is Javascripts relationship to HTML and CSS?

HTML (Hyper-text-markup-language) is the structure of your webpage. Think of it as the bones and muscles. HTML is a great starting point but not very exciting to look at.

CSS is the styling or 'look' of your webpage. Think of CSS as the design we place on those bones and muscles. With CSS you can have millions of different designs and thats just in the color department. Thinking back to our bones and muscles, CSS is the ablility to change the size and shape of those muscles as well as alter all sorts of other parts like the hair and eye color. CSS turns our boring HTML (or bones) into a webpage you 'want' to see and use.
